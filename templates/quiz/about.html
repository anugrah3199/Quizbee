ABOUT QUIZ-BEE

Quiz Bee is a web-based project for quiz practice.

This website mainly focus on the educational institute that can be college or school .

The motive behind our website is to provide a platform for the users to make a quiz online and provide the same to other user to attempt it.

Our main users are going to be professors in colleges can use our website to conduct the quiz and students who will attend quiz.

Professors can make their own quiz and can also maintain the progress of each of the student.
